Our Commitment to Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how HotelDeals collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you visit our website and use our services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information you voluntarily provide, such as:

  • Name and email address
  • Phone number
  • Mailing address
  • Payment information (processed securely)
  • Travel preferences and booking history

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our site, we automatically collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • IP address and location
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Device information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process bookings and payments
  • Send booking confirmations and updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Personalize your experience
  • Send promotional emails (with your consent)
  • Improve our services and platform
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and secure our platform

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Track affiliate performance
  • Analyze website usage
  • Improve user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of our site.

4. Data Sharing

Your information is shared with:

  • Trip.com: For booking processing and fulfillment
  • Payment Processors: For secure payment handling
  • Service Providers: For email, analytics, and hosting
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law

We never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure payment processing
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited employee access to personal data
  • Password protection and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Port your data to another service
  • Lodge a complaint with privacy authorities

7. Retention Period

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Resolve disputes
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Enforce our agreements

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete such information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by posting a prominent notice on our website. Your continued use of our services following changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
