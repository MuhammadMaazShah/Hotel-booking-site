What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit and improve your user experience.

Why We Use Cookies

HotelDeals uses cookies for several important purposes:

  • To make our website function properly
  • To remember your preferences and login information
  • To track affiliate referrals and commissions
  • To analyze how visitors use our site
  • To personalize your experience
  • To improve our services and content
  • To deliver targeted advertisements

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Duration: Session (until browser closes)

These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly. They enable basic functionality such as:

  • User authentication and login
  • Security features
  • Site navigation
  • Form data management

Analytics & Performance Cookies

Duration: 6-12 months

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • User behavior patterns
  • Traffic sources
  • Device and browser information

Tools: Google Analytics, Hotwire

Preference & Functionality Cookies

Duration: 12 months

These cookies remember your settings and preferences:

  • Language preferences
  • Display settings
  • Search history
  • Previous bookings

Affiliate & Tracking Cookies

Duration: 30-90 days

These cookies track affiliate performance and referrals:

  • Affiliate link clicks
  • Referral source
  • Conversion tracking
  • Commission calculations

Marketing & Advertising Cookies

Duration: Variable (1-12 months)

These cookies deliver personalized advertisements:

  • Retargeting ads
  • Interest-based advertising
  • Conversion tracking
  • Ad performance analytics

Third-Party Cookies

We allow certain third parties to place cookies on our site:

  • Trip.com: For booking and affiliate tracking
  • Google Analytics: For website analytics
  • Facebook Pixel: For advertising and analytics
  • Payment Processors: For secure transactions

These third parties have their own cookie policies and privacy statements.

Your Cookie Choices

Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality. Cannot be disabled.

All Other Cookies: You can control these through:

  • Browser Settings: Delete or disable cookies in your browser preferences
  • Cookie Banner: Use our cookie consent banner when you first visit
  • Opt-Out Tools: Visit Google Analytics opt-out extension

Note: Disabling cookies may affect your browsing experience and prevent certain features from working properly.

Browser Cookie Settings

Most browsers allow you to control cookies:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Delete cookies
  • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data

Cookie Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or our cookie practices. Continued use of our website after updates constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
